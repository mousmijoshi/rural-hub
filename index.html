🌱 About GramaDharini

What is GramaDharini?

GramaDharini (ग्रामधारिणी) means "one who supports the village". We are a free platform built specially for students from rural areas of India.

Many talented students in villages and small towns don't have access to the right opportunities. GramaDharini bridges that gap by bringing internships, free courses, resume tools, and competitions — all in one free platform.

Whether you are from a small village in Maharashtra, Rajasthan, or any other state — GramaDharini is for YOU.
